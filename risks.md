# Array Mbira Risks

- No tine geometry, tuning, or resonator dimension has been measured; treat
  every physical value in this packet as `pending_measurement` until a tine
  mule exists (see `design.md` Tine Mechanics / L2 Packet Gates).
- Interval mapping for the isomorphic grid is still an open design decision;
  committing to a layout before the ergonomic mockup risks unplayable reaches
  or awkward note duplication.
- Tine clamp/bridge design is unreviewed; inconsistent clamp pressure can
  cause pitch drift, buzz, or tine damage during service.
- Resonator topology (shared body vs. grouped vs. removable modules) is
  unselected; committing to a body/CAD design before resonator comparison
  risks uneven loudness or dead notes across the array.
- Remain at L2 until layout mockup, tine mule, bridge review, resonator
  comparison, and body interface gates (design.md "L2 Packet Gates") are
  measured and reviewed.
